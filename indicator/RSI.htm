Relative Strength Index (RSI)

Author
J. Welles Wilder

Reference
Book New Concepts in Technical Trading Systems [Amazon.com]
by J. Welles Wilder; Trend Research. ISBN:0894590278

Abstract
Price oscillator that ranges between 0 and 100. Divergence with the price is used to hint for a reversal.
